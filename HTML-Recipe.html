Irish Potatoes

This sweet treat is perfect for Saint Patricks day. It is an Irish tradition that everyone will love. If you like coconut and cream cheese then this is the recipe for you.

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup butter, soffened
  • 1/2 (8 ounces) cream cheese
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 4 cups confectioners' sugar
  • 2 1/2 cups flaked coconut
  • 1 1/2 tablespoon ground cinnamon

Directions

  1. Place the cream cheese, butter, vanilla, and salt in a stand mixing bowl. Beat for about 3 minutes on medium speed until fluffy and smooth. Stop the mixer and scrape down the sides of the bowl and the beater with a rubber spatula.
  2. Return the mixer to low speed and gradully add the powedered sugar. Once all of the powdered sugar is incorprated, increase the speed to medium and beat again for one minute untill smooth.
  3. Line a baking sheet with aluminum foil or parchment paper. Scoop he coconut mixture out by level tablespoons onto the baking sheet. They should look like little balls. Once all the mixture is used, refrigerate for 30 minutes.
  4. Place the cinnamon in a small bowl. Using your washed hands, roll 1 of the balls into the cinnamon until completley coated, then place it back on the baking sheet. Reapeat with the remaining balls.

This is a no bake recipe.

Enjoy this tasty treat on Saint Patricks Day with your friends and family!
